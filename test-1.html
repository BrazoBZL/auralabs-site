Peptides 101: What You Need to Know

What Are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules in your body. They trigger specific biological responses — from muscle growth to tissue repair to fat loss.

Why Peptides?

Peptides work WITH your body's natural systems, triggering your own hormone production. They're reversible, selective, and far safer than synthetic alternatives.

Peptides vs. Steroids

Peptides: Trigger natural production, reversible, fewer side effects.
Steroids: Synthetic, permanent damage risk, broad unwanted effects.

Safety First

Always consult a healthcare provider. Individual responses vary. Cycling recommended. Never mix peptides without understanding compatibility.
